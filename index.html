Tacos de Carne Asada

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flank Steak 1 lb (450g)
Olive Oil 2 tablespoons
Lime Juice 1/4 cup
Garlic 3 cloves, minced
Ground Cumin 1 teaspoon
Chili Powder 1 tablespoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Fresh Cilantro 1/4 cup, chopped
Tortillas 8 small
Onion 1, chopped
Cilantro (for garnish) 1/4 cup, chopped
Lime Wedges For serving

Step 1: Marinate the flank steak with olive oil, lime juice, garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the steak for 5-7 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.

Step 3: Remove the steak from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes. Slice thinly against the grain.

Step 4: Warm the tortillas on the grill or in a dry skillet.

Step 5: Serve the sliced steak in tortillas with chopped onion, fresh cilantro, and lime wedges.

Source: Love and Lemons

Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Ground Beef 500g
Onion 1 large, chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Tomato Sauce 400g
Tomato Paste 2 tablespoons
Beef Broth 1 cup
Italian Seasoning 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Pepper 1/4 teaspoon
Spaghetti 400g

Step 1: Cook the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.

Step 2: Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.

Step 3: Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, beef broth, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.

Step 4: Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain.

Step 5: Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.

Source: Simply Recipes
